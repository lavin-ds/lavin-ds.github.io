Daaru Pe Charcha

Jab karta hai dil mitaneko gam, tab karlo ek bottle daaru khatam,

Jab birthdays ho, ya kisi ki ho party, ya shaadi ho, ya dil ki barbaadi,

Jab kare dil manane ko jashan, tab mere yaar karle ek bottle daaru khatam.

Papa ne bheja tha hame karne padai, par class na jaakr humne badi daant khai, jab mann karta tha chal dete the hum bar, peete rehte the jabtak raat na hoti thi paar,

Ghaseet te, ghaseet te chaar saal ho gae pass, subject har saal alag the, par common tha daaru ka wo glass,

Ghar wale first sem me khareed de gae the dudh peene ke lie ye glass, par maine isme kabhi daaru ki jagah dudh pia ho iska nahi mujhe ehsaas,

College hua khatam, par khatam na hui daaru, aaj bhi yaaro ke sath rakhni padti hai jaam chalu

Kabhi lagta hai ki hume agar daaru ke alawa kuch kaam aur bhi hote, agar main maikhane jaakr har roz yun badnam na hota,

Mil jaati chahne wali hame bhi kahi raaho me, agar kadmon me nasha aur hath me jaam na hota.
