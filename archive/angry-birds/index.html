Angry Birds Catapulting Their Way Into Peoples Lives!

I didn’t get around to playing Angry Birds till it was released for Nokia phones. And I certainly did not understand what the hype about Angry birds was all about? That was half a year ago. Now I have just finished the 45 levels of the game in my phone thrice. Also played the Android version of the game. And am now playing Rio, the update to Angry Birds.

Since its release Angry Birds by Rovio games has gone on to break all mobile gaming records that have existed till now. Fans are downloading this game a million times a day! And Guinness Book of world records has recognized it as the “Top Paid-For App Store Game in Most Countries”.

With a projected 6.5 million paid downloads, no other video game comes close. It has also been the top paid download application in 67 countries since its release.

The popular game has grown “faster than any brand ever before,” quickly becoming a global phenomenon. The Angry Birds Rio version of the game has gone on to become the fastest downloaded game ever with 10 million downloads in 10 days.

Now Angry Birds merchandise is spreading from toys to cookbooks. People are dressing up as Birds. Mascots are trolling around campuses. You really have to admit this is one mobile gaming app that has a cult following. Well time to sign off now, gotta get back to the birds.
